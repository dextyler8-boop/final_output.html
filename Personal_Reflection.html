A Student’s Reflection as a Digital Citizen of TCGC

As a student of TCGC, I believe that learning is not only about academics but also about becoming a better person. Our school reminds us to live with faith, integrity, and compassion, even in the digital world where we spend much of our time.

“My faith guides me in how I act online. It reminds me to be kind, respectful, and thoughtful in everything I post or share.”

Integrity is important to me as a TCGC student. I choose to be honest with my work and my words. True learning comes from doing what is right. Integrity helps me build trust and grow as a person.

“Being a compassionate digital citizen means understanding that my actions online affect others. I try to think before I click, comment, or share.”

Through my actions online and offline, I hope to reflect the values of my school and become the person God calls me to be.

— Kate Jalem
