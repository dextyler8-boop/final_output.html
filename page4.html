Introduction

The internet is a powerful tool for learning, sharing, and connecting. Ethical digital practices ensure we interact online with respect, honesty, and kindness. By following these principles, we can make the digital world safer and more positive for everyone.

Key Ethical Guidelines

Online Kindness

Communicate respectfully, avoid cyberbullying, and support others positively.

Honesty in Creation
